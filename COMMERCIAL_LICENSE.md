# Label-Forge Commercial Licensing Overview

This file is a commercial licensing summary for the public Community Edition
repository. It is not a substitute for a signed commercial agreement.

## What the public Community Edition does not grant

A separate commercial or enterprise license is required for any of the following:

- Production use beyond the free Additional Use Grant defined in `LICENSE`
- OEM, embedding, redistribution, sublicensing, or white-label use
- Managed or hosted service use for third parties
- Access to private enterprise modules, signed builds, LTS packages, premium
  support channels, or enterprise-only deployment tooling
- Use of a version after its Change Date without complying with the AGPL change
  license terms

## What a commercial license can grant

A commercial or enterprise agreement may include some or all of the following:

- Expanded production-use rights
- Redistribution, OEM, or embedding rights
- White-label rights
- Private enterprise modules
- Air-gapped deployment packages
- SSO, advanced RBAC, audit, and support entitlements
- Version support windows, security backports, and SLA terms

## Data ownership

The software license does not claim ownership over customer input data or
customer-generated annotation outputs.

As between AItoAir, Inc. and the customer, unless a separate written agreement
says otherwise:

- Customer input images, videos, metadata, prompts, and model inputs remain the
  customer's data
- Annotation exports, review outputs, masks, tracks, and dataset exports remain
  the customer's output data

## Contact

Commercial licensing inquiries for Label-Forge Community Edition should be sent
to:

licensing@aitoair.com

When contacting AItoAir, Inc. about licensing, include:

- Legal name of the requesting organization
- Primary contact name, title, and reply email address
- Intended use of Label-Forge Community Edition
- Deployment type and scope, including environment count and expected named users
- Whether OEM, embedding, redistribution, white-label, or third-party hosted
  service rights are needed
- Target version or release window
- Any contract, procurement, security review, or support requirements

Do not include customer datasets, credentials, private model weights, or other
sensitive material in the initial inquiry.
